Works Cited Entries for Your Short Story Research Essay

Pre-formatted MLA entries for each assigned short story

How to Use This Page
  1. In the document mockup below, locate the author who wrote the story your essay is about.
  2. Select and copy only that entry — do not copy the others.
  3. Paste it into your research essay document alongside your three secondary source entries.
  4. Place it in the correct alphabetical position by the author’s last name.
  5. After pasting, check for formatting errors — hyperlinks, spacing, and italics may need to be fixed. See the How to Create Your Works Cited guide for step-by-step instructions.

Important: Correct MLA format is part of your essay grade. Any errors remaining on the Works Cited page will result in a reduction of points. Review your entry carefully after pasting.
