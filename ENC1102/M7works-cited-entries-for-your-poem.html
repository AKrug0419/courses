Works Cited Entries for Your Poem

Pre-formatted MLA 9 entries for all six research essay poem options.

How to Use This Page

Find Your Poem, Copy the Entry

All six poems available for this essay come from the Poetry Foundation website, which means the Works Cited format is the same for every option. Below you will find a completed MLA 9 entry for each poem, in the order they should appear on your Works Cited page (alphabetized by the poet's last name). Locate your poem, copy its entry exactly as it appears, and paste it into your Works Cited.

Before You Paste

Update the Accessed date. The date shown above is a placeholder. Replace "15 Oct. 2025" with the actual date on which you most recently opened your poem on the Poetry Foundation website.

Alphabetize. Place your poem's entry in alphabetical order alongside your three secondary source entries. Most of the time, that means by the poet's last name.

Keep the italics. The website name Poetry Foundation must remain italicized after you paste. If pasting strips the italics, reapply them by hand.
